Privacy Questions

Can PersonalAI be forced to reveal my data?

No. Since your data never leaves your device, there's nothing for us or anyone else to access. We don't have your data, so we can't be compelled to hand it over.

What about software updates?

Updates are delivered without any personal data collection. The update process doesn't access or transmit your personal information.

How do you improve the AI without my data?

We use public datasets and synthetic data for improvements. Your personal interactions remain completely private and are never used for development.

Can I verify these privacy claims?

Yes. Core privacy components are open source, and you can monitor network traffic to verify no personal data is transmitted.
